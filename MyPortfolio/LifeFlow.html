LifeFlow Project

A deep dive into design thinking, strategy, and user experience.



    LifeFlow is an elegantly designed (especially for mobile users) to help you take control of your day with clarity, focus, and balance. With intuitive layout and powerful tools, LifeFlow transforms your daily routine into a well-orchestrated flow of tasks, habits, and focus sessions.

Key Highlights of LifeFlow

    Real-time Command center A clean and visually appealing interface that shows the current time and date, creating a sense of awareness and presence.
    Task Management Made Simple The Today's Tasks panel allows you to easily add, track, and manage tasks for the day with just one click.
    Daily Habits Tracker Build and maintain consistency with the Daily Habits feature that monitors your progress percentage throughout the day.
    Quick Notes for Instant Ideas A Quick Notes section designed for jotting down spontaneous thoughts, reminders, or creative ideas, ensuring nothing gets lost.
