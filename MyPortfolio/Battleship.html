Battleship Project

A deep dive into design thinking, strategy, and user experience.



    A futuristic, browser-based Battleship game where players deploy their fleet, attack enemy waters, and challenge an AI opponent with adjustable difficulty levels. The game features dynamic stats, interactive 8x8 grids, and vibrant neon visuals with smooth animations for an engaging naval combat experience.

Game Features

    Interactive Gameplay Click-based attacks on an 8x8 grid with real-time updates.
    AI Opponent Three difficulty levels (Normal, Hard, Expert) with smart targeting on higher levels.
    Dynamic Statistics Tracks shots fired, hit accuracy, and battle turns.
    Legend & Feedback System Clear visual indicators for water, ships, hits, and misses.
