StepUp Project

A deep dive into design thinking, strategy, and user experience.



    Step Up is a cutting-edge futuristic shoe store that redefines the way you shop for footwear. Bring shoes to life with immersive 3D viewing technology, allowing you to rotate, zoom, and explore every detail as if you’re holding them in your hands. Sleek, modern interface is designed for ultimate accessibility, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable shopping experience for everyone. Whether you’re looking for the latest trends or timeless classics, Step Up combines innovation, style, and technology to help you find your perfect pair – like stepping into the future of fashion.

3D Interactive Product Viewer

    The centerpiece of the interface is a 3D model of a shoe, which users can rotate, zoom in, and view from all angles. This interactive experience allows customers to inspect the product details as if they were holding it in their hands.
